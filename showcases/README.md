# Showcase Demos

These demos are designed for first-time visitors.

They are intentionally dependency-free, deterministic, and easy to run. The goal is to show the shape of production-aware agent systems without requiring API keys.

## Demos

| Demo | What it shows | Run |
|---|---|---|
| [Healthcare Agent Colony](healthcare-agent-colony/README.md) | Safety boundaries and escalation | `python main.py` |
| [Finance Research Agent](finance-research-agent/README.md) | Research support without investment advice | `python main.py` |
| [Enterprise Support Agent](enterprise-support-agent/README.md) | Ticket routing and approval gates | `python main.py` |

## Why These Exist

People star repositories they can understand quickly and return to later.

These demos make the roadmap concrete:

- healthcare shows domain safety
- finance shows boundary-aware research
- enterprise shows workflow automation
